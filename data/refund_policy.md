# Refund Policy
**NexaCloud Support | Article ID: BIL-003 | Last Updated: 2025-06-01**

---

## Overview

NexaCloud's refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent. This document outlines when refunds are available, how to request them, and what to expect.

---

## 1. Money-Back Guarantee

### 14-Day Guarantee (New Subscriptions)
All new paid subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within **14 days** of the initial charge, provided:
- This is your first paid subscription (not a re-subscription)
- The request is made within 14 days of the initial charge date
- Your account is in good standing

**How to request:**
1. Email billing@nexacloud.io with subject: "Refund Request – [Your Account Email]"
2. Include your invoice number
3. Briefly describe why you're requesting a refund
4. Our team will process within 3–5 business days

### Annual Plan — 30-Day Guarantee
Annual plans have an extended **30-day** refund window from the initial charge date.

---

## 2. Standard Refund Scenarios

### Eligible for Refund
| Scenario | Refund Amount |
|---|---|
| New subscription within 14-day window | Full refund |
| Annual plan within 30-day window | Full refund |
| Duplicate charges (billing error) | Full refund of duplicate |
| Service outage exceeding SLA credits | SLA credit + optional refund review |
| Charged after cancellation | Full refund of post-cancellation charge |

### Not Eligible for Refund
| Scenario | Policy |
|---|---|
| Monthly subscription after 14 days | No refund |
| Annual subscription after 30 days | No refund (access continues until period end) |
| Downgrade from higher plan | No refund for unused portion |
| Unused features or low usage | No refund |
| Violation of Terms of Service | No refund, immediate suspension |
| Trial period charges | No charges during trial |

---

## 3. Exceptional Circumstances

NexaCloud may provide refunds at its discretion for:
- Significant technical issues caused by NexaCloud that prevented service use
- Major product changes that materially reduce value for your use case
- Account compromise due to NexaCloud security failure

These are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Contact billing@nexacloud.io with full documentation of the issue.

---

## 4. Refund Processing

### Timeline
| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit/Debit Card | 5–10 business days |
| UPI | 2–5 business days |
| Net Banking | 5–7 business days |
| Bank Transfer | 7–14 business days |

### How Refunds Are Issued
- Refunds are returned to the **original payment method**
- We do not issue refunds via bank transfer if the original payment was by card
- Credits cannot be converted to cash

### Confirmation
You will receive an email confirmation when:
1. Your refund request is received
2. The refund is approved and processed
3. The refund has been issued

---

## 5. Service Credits vs. Refunds

NexaCloud may offer **account credits** as an alternative to refunds in some cases:
- Credits are applied to future invoices
- Credits do not expire
- Credits are not transferable to other accounts
- You can choose credits or refunds where both are offered

---

## 6. Contacting Billing Support

For refund requests or billing disputes:
- **Email:** billing@nexacloud.io
- **Subject Line:** "Refund Request – [Invoice Number]"
- **Include:** Account email, invoice number, reason for request, preferred resolution

**Response time:** 1–2 business days for initial response.
