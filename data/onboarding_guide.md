# Getting Started with NexaCloud — Onboarding Guide
**NexaCloud Support | Article ID: ONB-001 | Last Updated: 2025-06-01**

---

## Welcome to NexaCloud

NexaCloud is an AI-powered SaaS platform for team collaboration, data analytics, and workflow automation. This guide walks you through everything you need to get up and running in under 30 minutes.

---

## 1. Account Setup Checklist

Use this checklist to complete your initial setup:

- [ ] Create and verify your account
- [ ] Set up your organization profile
- [ ] Configure security settings (MFA)
- [ ] Invite your first team members
- [ ] Create your first project
- [ ] Connect your first integration
- [ ] Explore the dashboard

---

## 2. Creating Your Account

### Sign Up
1. Visit [app.nexacloud.io/signup](https://app.nexacloud.io/signup)
2. Enter your work email, name, and choose a strong password
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete the onboarding wizard (takes ~5 minutes)

### Onboarding Wizard
The wizard will ask you to:
- Enter your organization name and industry
- Indicate your team size
- Select your primary use case (Analytics / Collaboration / Automation)
- Choose your timezone
- Optionally invite teammates

---

## 3. Setting Up Your Organization Profile

1. Navigate to **Settings → Organization**
2. Fill in:
   - **Organization Name:** Your company or team name
   - **Organization Slug:** Unique identifier used in URLs (e.g., `nexacloud.io/org/your-slug`)
   - **Industry:** Helps us suggest relevant templates
   - **Website:** Optional
   - **Logo:** Upload a square PNG/JPEG, min 256×256px
3. Save changes

---

## 4. Security Configuration

### Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) — Recommended
1. Go to **Settings → Security → Multi-Factor Authentication**
2. Click **"Enable MFA"**
3. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator
4. Enter the 6-digit code to verify
5. Save your **backup codes** in a secure location (you will not see them again)

### Require MFA for All Users (Admin)
1. Go to **Settings → Security → Organization Security Policy**
2. Toggle **"Require MFA for all members"**
3. Users will be prompted to set up MFA on their next login

---

## 5. Inviting Team Members

### Send Invitations
1. Go to **Settings → Users & Permissions**
2. Click **"Invite User"**
3. Enter email addresses (one per line for bulk invite)
4. Select roles (see Account Management guide for role descriptions)
5. Click **"Send Invitations"**

### Invitation Limits by Plan
| Plan | Max Team Members |
|---|---|
| Starter | 3 |
| Professional | 20 |
| Business | 100 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

---

## 6. Creating Your First Project

1. Click **"+ New Project"** on the home dashboard
2. Or navigate to **Projects → New Project**
3. Enter:
   - **Project Name**
   - **Description** (optional but recommended)
   - **Visibility:** Private or Internal to your organization
4. Click **"Create"**

### Adding Content to Your Project
- Click **"+ Add"** within the project
- Choose from: Dashboards, Data Sources, Reports, Workflows, Notes
- Follow the setup wizard for each content type

## 7. Connecting Your First Integration

NexaCloud connects with 50+ tools. Popular integrations:

| Tool | Use Case |
|---|---|
| Google Analytics | Web traffic data |
| Salesforce | CRM and sales data |
| Slack | Notifications and alerts |
| PostgreSQL / MySQL | Database connections |
| Google Sheets | Spreadsheet import |
| REST API | Custom data sources |

### Connecting an Integration
1. Go to **Settings → Integrations**
2. Find your tool in the list or search
3. Click **"Connect"**
4. Authorize NexaCloud in the third-party login
5. Configure sync settings (frequency, data scope)
6. Click **"Save & Sync"**

## 8. Navigating the Dashboard

### Main Navigation
- **Home:** Overview of recent activity, pinned projects
- **Projects:** All your projects and workspaces
- **Dashboards:** Data visualization views
- **Reports:** Scheduled and ad-hoc reports
- **Workflows:** Automated workflows and triggers
- **Settings:** Account, billing, integrations, security

### Keyboard Shortcuts
| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `Ctrl+K` | Global command search |
| `Ctrl+N` | New item (context-sensitive) |
| `Ctrl+/` | Show all shortcuts |
| `G then H` | Go to Home |
| `G then P` | Go to Projects |

## 10. Common First-Week Questions

**Q: How do I import existing data?**
A: Go to Settings → Data → Import. Supported formats: CSV, JSON, Excel, Google Sheets.

**Q: Can I try all features before committing to a paid plan?**
A: Yes — a 14-day trial of the Professional plan is included with all new accounts.

**Q: What happens if I reach my seat limit?**
A: You'll receive a warning. Upgrade your plan or remove inactive users.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes — see our Data Security Policy (SEC-001) for full details on encryption, compliance, and data handling.
