## 3. Trial Period

All new accounts receive a **14-day free trial** of the Professional plan:
- No credit card required for trial
- Full access to Professional features
- Auto-downgrade to Starter after 14 days if no payment
- Upgrade anytime during or after the trial

## 4. Upgrading Your Plan

### Immediate Upgrade
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Subscription**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Select the new plan
4. Review the prorated charge for the remainder of the current billing period
5. Confirm upgrade — takes effect immediately

### What Changes Immediately
- New feature set becomes available
- Increased limits applied instantly
- Prorated charge billed for the current period's difference

## 5. Downgrading Your Plan

### Process
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Subscription**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select the lower plan
4. Confirm — change takes effect at the end of the current billing period

### Things to Note When Downgrading
- Features not available in the lower plan are disabled at the downgrade date
- Data that exceeds the lower plan's limits is not deleted but becomes inaccessible
- You will be prompted to remove excess team members, projects, or data sources
- Downgrading from annual to monthly is not available mid-cycle

## 6. Plan FAQ

**Q: Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?**
A: Yes — go to Settings → Billing → Subscription and click "Switch to Annual". You'll receive the 20% discount and your billing cycle resets.

**Q: Are there discounts for nonprofits or startups?**
A: Yes — NexaCloud for Good program offers 50% off for registered nonprofits. Startup program available for early-stage companies. Apply at nexacloud.io/for-good.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my API call limit?**
A: API calls above your plan limit are charged at ₹0.01 per additional 1,000 calls. You'll receive an alert at 80% of your limit.

**Q: Can I have multiple subscriptions for different teams?**
A: No — each organization has one subscription. For multiple independent teams, create separate organizations.

**Q: Is pricing the same globally?**
A: INR pricing applies for India. USD/EUR pricing is available for other regions. Contact sales for regional pricing.
